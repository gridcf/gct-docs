[[myproxy-developer-archdes]]
== Architecture and design overview ==


--
The MyProxy system architecture and design is described in the following
two publications: 


* J. Basney, M. Humphrey, and V. Welch. http://www.ncsa.illinois.edu/People/jbasney/myproxy-spe.pdf[The MyProxy Online Credential Repository]. Software: Practice and Experience, 2005.

* J. Novotny, S. Tuecke, and V. Welch. http://toolkit.globus.org/alliance/publications/papers/myproxy.pdf[An Online Credential Repository for the Grid: MyProxy]. Proceedings of the Tenth International Symposium on High Performance Distributed Computing (HPDC-10), IEEE Press, August 2001.
